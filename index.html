TRAIL Project Status

Project Objective

The objective of Project TRAIL is to gather Road Infrastructure data across 100 Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities in India (both rural and urban), use AI-based Modeling Techniques to detect Road Assets, and map the results onto a web-based dashboard with GPS location markings.

Start Date

March 2024

Principal Investigators

Dr. Anbu Subramanian / Mr. Govind Krishnan

Projected Timelines

  • May 2024: Beta; 4 assets in 6 cities, model run for 10 min footage per city (Completed) - "Beta" refers to an initial test phase where the model was run on limited data to evaluate performance.
  • July 2024: 4 assets in 6 cities with improved accuracy (Completed)
  • September 2024: 6 assets in 6 cities (Completed)
  • December 2024: 5 assets in 10 cities (Completed)
  • January 2025: 10 assets in 10 cities (On track)
  • March 2025: 20 cities, 10 assets (Dependent on data capture)
  • June 2025: 50 cities, 15 assets
  • December 2025: 100 cities, 25 assets
  • March 2026: 100 cities, 30 assets
